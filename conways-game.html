Conway's Game

Rules Ref.

  • Any live cell with two or three living neighbours survives (stays alive.)
  • Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell (comes back to life.)
  • All other live cells die in the next generation. Similarly, all dead cells stay dead.
